Short Bio:

I work for the US Environmental Protection Agency's Atlantic Ecology Divsion where I study lakes and cyanobacteria. I am a landscape ecologist by training. The primary focus of my work is developing tools (GIS, R, Python etc.) to advance our groups research.

Why I am taking this class:

A big part of my job is combining datasets from multiple sources to answer questions pretaining to nutrients in lakes. Increasingly these datasets are served up as web services. Additionally, I am an Open Science advocate and believe that the results of my research should also be be made freely available and be easily discoverable. As such a solid and fundamental understanding of modern web technologies is essential. I am taking this class (as well as others) to build this foundation and go beyond just being self-taught hacker.
